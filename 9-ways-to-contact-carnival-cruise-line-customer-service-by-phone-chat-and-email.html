9 Ways to Contact Carnival Cruise Line Customer Service by Phone, Chat, and Email

Carnival Cruise Line is one of the world’s leading cruise companies, known for its exceptional service and unforgettable vacation experiences. Whether you're booking a cruise, managing an existing reservation, or resolving a travel issue, this guide will help you connect with Carnival Cruise Line customer service easily.

1. Contact Carnival Cruise Line by Phone (Fastest Option)

The quickest way to get help is by calling their customer service directly. Representatives can assist with:

  • Booking new cruises
  • Managing or modifying reservations
  • Cruise documentation and travel requirements
  • Onboard experiences
  • Lost and found
  • Refunds and cancellations

Official Phone Number: 1-833-320-0080

Have your reservation number and personal details ready for faster service. Support is available during regular business hours and often on weekends.

2. Live Chat Support

If you prefer not to call, you can chat with a representative online. Live chat is ideal for:

  • Instant answers to basic questions
  • Multitasking while getting support

To start a chat, visit the Carnival Cruise Line website and click on the “Chat” icon at the bottom of the page.

For complex issues like refunds, booking errors, or name corrections, calling is still recommended.

3. Email Support

If your issue is not urgent, email is a good option. Use the contact forms on their official contact page to submit your inquiry. Response times may take 1–3 business days.

4. Social Media and Mobile Support

You can also reach out through Carnival Cruise Line's official social media accounts on Facebook, X (formerly Twitter), and Instagram. While DMs are fine for general queries, always call for booking or urgent concerns.

5. Common Reasons to Call Customer Service

  • Booking a cruise
  • Changing cruise dates
  • Canceling a trip
  • Lost luggage
  • Travel insurance issues
  • Future Cruise Credits (FCC)
  • Accessibility requests

Use 1-833-320-0080 for all major support needs.

6. Customer Service Hours

  • Monday to Friday: 9 AM – 10 PM (ET)
  • Saturday & Sunday: Limited hours

Check your local time zone and be prepared for potential hold times during peak hours.

7. Final Tips

  • Use the phone for anything urgent: 1-833-320-0080
  • Use live chat for quick, basic questions
  • Email for non-urgent or document-related concerns
  • Save the customer service number for future reference
